About Me:



I am a PhD Research Fellow working on formal methods and privacy at the University of Oslo. My PhD work mainly focuses on designing programming languages to enforce privacy and security policies. I authored several research papers in designing an object-oriented language for GDPR compliant systems, runtime verification, and operational semantics specification using rewriting logics. During my PhD, I went for research visits and collaborated with internationally leading researchers. I have completed MSc in computer networks. In my MSc's project, I worked with machine learning algorithms to design an intrusion detection system.

After my long journey in academia, I have decided to join the industry and tackle real-world problems! I love developing software solutions and learning algorithms for AI systems. I enjoy coding and crushing code bugs! :) I love teamwork, teaching, and learning new skills. I think it is fun to learn a new skill and apply it into projects and see that it is actually working. I like this dynamic in the industry work.
